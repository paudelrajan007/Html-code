Steven Paul Jobs: Biography, Philosophy, and Lessons

Biography

Steven Paul Jobs (February 24, 1955 – October 5, 2011) was an American.Born in San Francisco and adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs. He grew up in Silicon Valley.

In 1976, Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne launched Apple from a garage.

Philosophy on the World

* Think Different 
* Mortality as Motivation: He urge people to “stay hungry, stay foolish”

Lessons from His Life

*Simplify 
    *Live Purposefully: “Your time is limited; don’t waste it living someone else’s life.” Pursue passion over convention.
